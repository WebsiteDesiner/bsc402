‚ö° X402 Protocol Features

The X402 protocol introduces several innovative features that set it apart from traditional token distribution models:

  • Direct Minting: Users can mint tokens by sending BNB directly to the contract address
  • Fixed Rate: 1 BNB = 50,000,000 BNB402 tokens
  • Minimum Contribution: 0.001 BNB per transaction
  • Maximum Supply: 10,000 mints total
  • Automatic Liquidity: At 10 BNB collected, liquidity is automatically added to PancakeSwap

üí± How to Mint

Minting BNB402 tokens is simple and straightforward:

1. Connect your BSC-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
2. Ensure you have BNB for gas fees
3. Choose the number of batches (1 batch = 0.001 BNB)
4. Click "Batch Request Mint API"
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. Receive BNB402 tokens instantly
